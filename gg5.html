Shower Thoughts

The pain begins, please let it go well.

Mommy is cleaning and wiping and mopping everything again. Nothing is changing. If anything, I'm getting even worse. At least I didn’t scream today, but…maybe...maybe I should kill myself. Not today, since I have a new episode to watch tonight. Not anytime soon. So much left to do, so many thoughts. But I'm not even taking any action. So paradoxical, this excuse to keep on living. Like a bloody leech. Disgusting. I might make a speech about that someday, at least, as a failure or something else. A performance to reveal.

 

Now that I am not convenient to work on my thoughts, I ironically have so many ideas I want to write about.

'It is only in discomfort that we regret not properly appreciating comfort, yet it is so much easier to find even more discomfort in times of comfort.’ That sounds pretty good, doesn't it?

Or...‘It is only during the times when we are more inconvenient that we experience something that we believe we could appreciate better if only we are a little bit more convenient’.

I wonder if I might remember that when I get out. ‘Order provokes disorder, and vice-versa’.

 

There aren't any prayers from the mosque. Is it four now? Five? There should be...Did they skip it again today? Why do they keep doing that? The next one is half past seven. Screw it, it might already be six now. I need to be quicker. I keep forgetting to get a clock. Something is making me forget every time I am outside my rituals.

 

Was that a damn bubble? Or was it an insect? The flight trajectory was too straight. Could it be a trick of the light? A reflection on my glasses? Maybe I was just hallucinating. But was it a damn bloody bubble?

It is all just in my cognition. It doesn’t matter. It won’t matter tomorrow anyway, if I could just ignore it now, if I could convince myself that I was simply imagining that movement, if I could get myself to forget about it. My mental stability is weak; I can alter my memories, for the sake of getting through this. I can. I can. I can.

Pretend that I didn’t notice it. Pretend that it didn’t happen.

It makes sense though. I just used some soap on my feet, and maybe I scrubbed too recklessly. But it disappeared before I could take a proper look at it, and my glasses are always very reflective, and my imagination is usually wild. And it was floating too fast to be a bubble. But I’m also using hot water, so it’s logical that it goes up quicker.

Is this cognitive dissonance? Am I rationalising? Am I rationalising against or in favour of the bubble’s existence? Am I just thinking about what I want to think? What in the bloody hells do I even want to think about? Bah.

Procrastinate thinking about this. I’m good at procrastination. I’m a bloody expert. Think about that game. Or that movie. Something else. Or don’t think at all. Bear with it. An hour later and it won’t matter as much.

But what if…? What if-…No, it’s in everyone’s best interest to shut up. Shut up. Shut up.

But that bubble. Bloody hell. One more such mishap and my composure might be dashed.

 

If a genie comes, should I wish to disappear or for everything to be fixed? I don't think it makes a difference whichever I choose.

Wait, that was pretty poetic.

 

‘The crown with the highest efficiency convinces the world that it rests upon the head of a totalitarian and authoritarian autocracy, but in reality acts otherwise. Tell them to be afraid and show them they should rightly be, but shower them with love; that is the basis of ruling.’ Axhra? Yeah, Axhra's quote.

 

When I do get out, and if the ritual goes smoothly-Ah, yes! Let me flip a butterfly coin: If the ritual goes swimmingly without any twists or mishaps, I would definitely play the piano tonight. This time, for sure! I would also reconnect with her by sending an opening message. Maybe her as well. And it has been some time since I talked with him. Oh no, I still owe them money for that cake. It’s been three months…

Bloody heavens, I have so much left undone. That bloody bank account, and the luggage, and my opinions on...Never mind them. What matters now is making sure the ritual isn't screwed over. And it's not like doing just that would be simple. Everything else can wait for after, when my mind is less occupied.

How I wish I am by my computer now, finished with this tedious ritual. I promise I would better appreciate it this time. I would savour the night sky and I would write a lot. Maybe I just might.

Please, no bubbles, no water rebounds, no insects. Please.

 

‘Flip a butterfly coin’, huh? Making decisions by seeing what randomly happens; seeing where causality leads. Cool. I might add that to my world.

If only my thoughts could be automatically recorded, I wouldn't need to search so hard.

If only everyone’s thoughts could be printed out instantly, what a wonderful world it’d be. Well, wonderful in the long run. It would also be a relief to see if everyone’s mind is as jumbled as mine. I wonder what they think about?

 

Why is the water pressure so bloody low? Damn human inventions.

‘And I think it's gonna be a long long time, till touchdown brings me round again to find, I'm not the man they think I am at home, oh no no no, I'm a rocketman!’ Bloody hell, that was close, damn rebounds. I should stop singing. Concentrate.

A song about drug addiction, by the by. Fitting, right? I must learn it by piano. Maybe tonight. Everything is going well so far. Too well. Did I accidentally make a mistake somewhere that my mind overlooked?

 

Damn the heavens, the sky is darkening. Has so much time already passed? Maybe it is just the clouds. No, I need to move faster, or else it would be really late when I finish.

Oh, what is the bloody point? Why must I rush when time insists on rushing on even faster? I'll spare myself the hassle.

No, that won't do. Mommy needs to sleep early, and I need to spare some time for any extra mishaps that might happen on my way out.

I still have one, no…there’s an hour for this and another for that and then…I’m so tired, and hungry, and sleepy, and lazy. And bloody pissed. Maybe…Maybe I should-No. I went over that already just now. Stop repeating.

Please, no ants, no black dots. Don't slip. Oh, but if I do slip, do off me right away. No, wait, that’d be traumatic. Excuses. Excuses. Shut up.

High bloody Heavens, four minutes of prayer immediately if I get out safely. No bloody twists, or else you all get nothing.

 

‘Up on his tower of marble, his legs dangling, the man observes the plague-stricken city, a golden chalice of wine in hand.’

But I know I am meant for much more. This mind of mine, if only it was granted to someone with better...something. Whatever, I can ponder on this some other time. I just...

Bloody water pressure!

'True unluckiness is the inability to use the luck already given'. Ha!

It doesn’t matter. Nothing does. Shut up.

 

Goodbye: um...‘Goodfaring’. ‘Wellfaring’. That's not that bad.

‘Time is like destiny. If time could be changed, it essentially would not’. Aesena's eventual understanding, right? Hm, I think it still needs a bit more clarity. Time-travelling is a hard plot to tackle, but mine is the only way it properly makes sense.

I will write with Aesena tonight. I think I will. I just need to explain it well.

 

A story about three goldfishes in a bowl: one who thinks the bowl is the world, one who thinks it was put there by The Creator, and one who knows their true reality. It could be a famous literary novel in my world. I wonder how I can put that to use. But…which one am I?

 

Speech-shortcuts: a word that means a paragraph. Like a function button. ‘EILWAL’: ‘Do not respond to this that I am displaying here, and do not bring it up in the future’. That’s a useful sentiment. ‘GARZIS’: ‘I apologise, but something you said is reminding me that I have to think about something else for a while, so let me zone out’. Ooh, a pretty good one. I’m going to use that so much.

‘MIERDA’: ‘The silence is lengthening, let me contribute a random topic’. This idea should be an actual thing. For my world then.

 

Script-conversing: A preparation of a written script for a verbal conversation to allow navigation through awkward topics, both people reading their own lines turn-by-turn from start to finish so that the overall idea is delivered and expressed without unexpected interjections or interruptions. Of course, it must take into account both characters' personalities and any predictably characteristic opinions. It's an amazing idea for couples. I might give it to...Axhra? No, he has a little bit too many already. Zekith then, Zekith and Luena, I will give it to them.

It’s also brilliant if I could implement it on my own friendships, as an icebreaker too maybe. It’s risky, though. Nah, everyone knows I’m crazy anyway.

 

Too many ideas. Goldfish, script, coin, um...that's all, right? No, hold on. Oh no. Bloody hell.

 

I know. Yes, finally, I know what I am going to do tonight. I know what I want to write.

I want to show them. Let them know what is going on inside my head. Let them judge me. Let them pity me. Let everyone know. Hide nothing. Tell them that they don’t know me. Shock them. Intrigue them. Disappoint them. Impress them. Unnerve them. Disgust them. I don’t care.

Let them understand. Or let them misunderstand. Let them think they understand. Let them ignore it. It doesn’t matter. I just have to show them. Let them be aware of who I really am.

Let them tell everyone else, so I don’t need to do it myself. Let it spread. Or let it not. Maybe it might trigger a change. Maybe not. It is a good read. It is a bad read. I don’t give a…

I do. I don’t. No difference. No…

I know what to write.
